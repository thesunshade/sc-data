• Numerical Discourses 15.4

Two Kinds of Work

SC 1Thus I have heard: One time, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove of Śrāvastī.

SC 2It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the monks, “There are two kinds of work. What are the two? They are Dharma work and material work. Monks, the best kind of work doesn’t go beyond the Dharma work.

SC 3Therefore, monks, you should always train in Dharma work and not in material work. Thus, monks, you should train yourselves.”

SC 4When the monks heard what the Buddha taught, they rejoiced and approved.
